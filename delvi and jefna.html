Delvin & Jefna

A story that quietly began during the silent days of the pandemic

Delvin never expected that the quietest days of the COVID-19 pandemic would become the beginning of the most beautiful chapter of his life.

The world had suddenly slowed down. Schools closed, streets were empty, and every day felt strangely quiet. For Delvin, life had become a routine of online classes, silent afternoons, and long nights with his phone.

One evening, during an online class discussion with his classmates, something small happened.

In the background of the call, a soft voice appeared.

“Chechi… give me the phone for a minute.”

Delvin paused.

He knew that voice.

It was Jefna — his classmate’s younger sister. Before the pandemic, he had seen her many times in the school corridors.

“Hi,” he would say.

“Hi,” she would reply.

That was all their story was back then.

But hearing her voice through the phone somehow felt different.

“Hi Delvin,” she said casually.

“Hey… bored at home?” he asked.

“Very.”

That conversation lasted only a few minutes.

But the next day she appeared again.

And the day after that.

Slowly, something unexpected began to happen.

The girl who was once just a familiar face in school had now become a daily voice in his life.

Days quietly turned into weeks.

Weeks slowly became months.

They talked about everything—music, school memories, dreams about the future, random jokes that made no sense but still made them laugh.

“You should listen to me,” Delvin said proudly.

“Why?” Jefna asked.

“I’m older. I have more experience.”

“Two years doesn’t make you wise, Delvin.”

Somewhere between those endless conversations, something quietly changed between them.

But before they could fully understand it, life moved forward again.

The pandemic slowly ended. Schools reopened.

Delvin moved to higher secondary, later leaving his hometown to study in another state.

Meanwhile, Jefna finished her higher secondary education and joined a German language institute, preparing for her dream of studying in Germany.

Years passed quietly.

One evening, Delvin received a message from an unknown number.

“Hi… I’m Jefna’s friend from the institute.
Day after tomorrow is Jefna’s birthday.
Can you come?”

Two days later, Delvin arrived with a small box of chocolates in his hand.

Chocolate had always been one of Jefna’s favorite things.

Then he saw her.

Jefna was standing there with her friends.

She was wearing a light blue full-length frock, gently flowing in the evening breeze. Across the dress were small dark blue butterfly designs that seemed to move whenever she walked.

The soft color of the dress made her look calm and graceful, and for a moment Delvin simply stood there, unable to move.

She looked… beautiful.

“Delvin…?”

“Happy birthday.”

Later he discovered something surprising.

The message from “her friend” was actually sent by Jefna herself.

She had secretly used her friend’s phone to text him.

Because she too had been waiting for a reason to see him again.

Years had passed. Cities had changed. Distances had grown.

But somehow… a small voice from those silent lockdown days had still found its way back to him.

And maybe… their story was only beginning. 🦋
